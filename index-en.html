Our Path

The Non-Profit Organization "A OPOS AGAPI" was founded on the principles of selfless offering and social solidarity. Our mission is to support every person in need, creating a chain of love that knows no borders.

Blackboard

"Blackboard" is our educational pillar. We provide support to students and families, ensuring that the right to knowledge and creation is accessible to everyone, regardless of their circumstances.

Our Actions

From food distribution to educational seminars, our actions are designed to have a direct impact on the lives of our fellow citizens.
